Video edting

Story

I began video editing while pursuing Engineering studies a few years ago. I started off by making funny compilations of my friends doing stupid things, which I then share as a birthday surprise. However, I discovered Adobe Premier Pro and began to enjoy the flow of working on ideas

I have been inspired by vlogs and VFX content on Youtube and Instagram by the likes of Zack King ,Sam Kolder, Cache_bunny and Peter McKinnon to name a few.

I have tried out a lot of VFX and editing tricks since then, the best of which I post on Instagram. I also began editing wedding and other function videos as freelancing projects. I feel editing videos helps me enter a flow state, which definitely makes me believe that this is one of my Ikigai.
